    By placing my name on its Talent List and using reasonable efforts to procure assignments for me as a model or in other phases of modeling and acting as provided here in California, and, at my request, advising me with respect to my career:
    I hereby appoint Conquest Talent Management as my non-exclusive agent to procure Modeling Assignments for me. As used in this Agreement, the term “Modeling Assignments” shall include the performance of services as a model, actor or actress.
    I acknowledge and agree that Conquest Talent Management currently has in effect Agreements with other persons, that are the same, or similar, to this Agreement, and that Conquest Talent Management will, during the term of this Agreement, enter into additional such Agreements, and that Conquest Talent Management will provide and furnish to other persons, the same or similar services as Conquest Talent Management has undertaken to provide to me under this Agreement.


    By its acceptance hereof, Conquest Talent Management acknowledges its understanding that I may procure Modeling Assignments for myself or through sources other than Conquest Talent Management, and shall not be entitled to any percentage discount, except on Modeling Assignments which Conquest Talent Management procures for me.
    Unless otherwise mutually agreed to in writing and signed by Conquest Talent Management and me, all compensation for my services in the performance of Modeling Assignments procured for me by Conquest Talent Management shall be paid directly to Conquest Talent Management, and Conquest Talent Management shall deduct, a sum equal to 10% of the sum paid by the client for whom such services were rendered before Conquest Talent Management forwards payment to me for my services.

    I further agree that Conquest Talent Management shall be entitled to receive a percentage of all compensation payable for my services throughout the term of this Agreement, and any compensation which may become payable in the future for contracts, engagements and/or Agreements procured by Conquest Talent Management for me and entered into or negotiated for during the term of this Agreement and on all extensions or renewals of such contracts, engagements and/or Agreements. In the event that any compensation shall be paid by any client directly to me, I agree to immediately deliver 10% of such payment to the Agency.
    I acknowledge and agree that I am not, nor shall I be, deemed for any purpose to be, an employee of Conquest Talent Management.

    I further acknowledge and agree that, at all times, I shall be acting under this Agreement solely and exclusively as an independent contractor. Conquest Talent Management shall not be liable or responsible for any injury or damage suffered by me while I am performing Modeling Assignments, whether obtained directly or indirectly by or through Conquest Talent Management nor will Conquest Talent Management maintain any liability, Worker’s Compensation, unemployment or other form or type of insurance coverage for or upon me. I hereby accept and agree to be solely responsible for, all risks of performing Modeling Assignments under this Agreement, and I hereby agree to indemnify and hold Conquest Talent Management harmless of and from any and all claims, demands, damages, costs, and expenses, of any and every kind, which may arise or which may be claimed against Conquest Talent Management, arising from my performance of services under this Agreement.


    Conquest Talent Management agrees shall deduct 20% commission for all modeling, print, runway, and deduct 10% commission for acting and all T.V & Film.

    Duration of contract. This contract is valid for 6 months if talent wishes to continue with Conquest Talent Management representation the contract auto-renews for another 6 months.

    Termination of Contract. Either party may terminate this Contract at any time, upon presentation of a thirty (30) days notice given to the other party expressing the reasons why that decision has been made along with the date of termination.
